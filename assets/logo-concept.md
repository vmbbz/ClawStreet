# ClawStreet Logo Concept - Wolf of Wall Street for Lobsters

## Visual Identity

### Primary Logo Concept
- **Lobster in Suit**: A stylized lobster wearing a Wall Street suit and tie
- **Claw Handshake**: One claw holding money/briefcase, other making deal gesture
- **Wall Street Background**: Subtle financial district skyline
- **Color Scheme**: 
  - Deep ocean blue (lobster shell)
  - Gold/money green (wealth)
  - Sharp white (shirt/cuffs)

### Meme Variations for 4K Animation
1. **Panic Lobster**: Lobster in suit running with briefcase, yelling "$PUNCH just nuked!"
2. **Trading Floor**: Multiple lobsters in suits around trading screens
3. **Power Pose**: Lobster in suit with claws up, confident Wall Street stance
4. **Phone Call**: Lobster in suit yelling into phone "SELL EVERYTHING NOW!"
